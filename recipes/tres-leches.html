Tres Leches

Description

Tres Leches cake is a traditional Latin American dessert that is beloved for its moist and spongy texture, creamy and sweet flavor, and luxurious topping of whipped cream. This cake is made by soaking a sponge cake in a mixture of three different kinds of milk: whole milk, sweetened condensed milk, and evaporated milk. The result is a tender and moist cake that is infused with a rich and creamy sweetness. The whipped cream on top adds a lightness and freshness that perfectly balances the richness of the cake. Tres Leches cake is typically served cold, making it a refreshing and indulgent dessert that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12-ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour and baking powder.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. While the cake is baking, prepare the tres leches mixture. In a large bowl, whisk together the whole milk, sweetened condensed milk, and evaporated milk.
  9. When the cake is finished baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  10. Using a fork, poke holes all over the top of the cake. Slowly pour the tres leches mixture over the cake, making sure to fill the holes.
  11. Cover the cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  12. Before serving, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the top of the cake.
  13. Sprinkle cinnamon over the top of the whipped cream, if desired.
